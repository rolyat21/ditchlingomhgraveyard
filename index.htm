Ditchling Unitarians


We welcome all who seek the meaning of life. We welcome all who believe that religion is wider than any one sect and deeper than any one set of opinions.

We seek exploration of all possible truths from all possible sources. We have respect for Christian tradition coupled with a modern religious outlook which encourages freedom of individual thought and belief. Our fellowship provides strength and encouragement for daily living.

Our Minister, Rev. Duncan Voice, is available most Wednesdays between 10am -12.30pm. You are welcome to make an appointment or pop in for a chat.

Our small and friendly meditation group welcomes new members. We practice mindfulness meditation together once a month. See Activities section for dates.
